About Him

from the day he won the world cup

Lionel Andrés Messi Cuccittini, also known as Leo Messi, is an Argentine football player who plays for Major League Soccer club Inter Miami and is the captain of the Argentina national team.

His Trophies

from the day he won the world cup

There are 39 trophies in his club career. He has won La Liga 10 times, Spanish Super Cup 8 times, Copa del Rey 7 times, Champions League 4 times, UEFA Super Cup 3 times, Fifa Club World Cup 3 times, Ligue 1 twice and French Cup once. In his national team career FIFA U-20 World Cup - Gold Medal, Copa América - Silver Medal, Summer Olympics - Gold Medal, FIFA World Cup - Silver Medal, Copa América - Silver Medal, Copa América - Bronze Medal, Copa América - Gold Medal, Finalissima - Gold Medal, FIFA World Cup - Won Gold Medal
